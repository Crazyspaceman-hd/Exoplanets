WHERE

This page contains charts relating to stars where exoplanets were found.
This bar chart divides all planets from our data set by the spectral type of the star they are orbiting.
This histogram divides all stars with discovered exoplanets by age, it contains 20 bins and skews heavily towards younger stars though there is an outlier system at over 14 billion years old!
This histogram divides all stars with discovered exoplanets by distance, it contains 40 bins in an effort to deal with outlier systems over 4000 parsecs away. A parsec is a unit of distance equal to 3.26 light years.

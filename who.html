WHO

This page contains charts relating to the facilities that discovered the exoplanets.
This first plot splits the exoplanets by the location of the facility that discovered them between earth based or space based telescopes. While the ground telescopes have discovered more exoplanets overall there are 6 times the number of ground facilities that have been used for discovery indicating the importance of space based observation platforms.
At first glance this chart may not seem to be overly helpful however it helps to illustrate the dominance of space based observatories over their ground counterparts. The Kepler space telescope left service in 2018 after less than 10 years but at this point remains far ahead of it's closest ground based competitor.
